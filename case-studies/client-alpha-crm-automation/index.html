The Challenge

Revenue Leaking Through Manual CRM Operations

Client Alpha, a mid-market B2B software company, was generating strong inbound demand but failing to convert leads efficiently. Their sales team was spending 40% of their time on non-selling activities: data entry, lead research, CRM updates, and follow-up sequencing.

The result was predictable: slow response times, inconsistent follow-up, poor data quality, and deals falling through the cracks. They estimated that 30% of qualified leads received no meaningful follow-up within 48 hours.

Key pain points identified:

  • Manual lead qualification consuming 15 hours per rep per week
  • CRM data quality below 60% accuracy, making forecasting unreliable
  • Inconsistent follow-up cadences resulting in deal slippage
  • No automated enrichment or research on inbound leads
  • Sales leadership had zero visibility into pipeline health in real-time
The Solution

Autonomous AI Agents for Revenue Operations

Augentic AI designed and deployed a multi-agent AI system that automated the entire front-end of Client Alpha's revenue operations. The system integrated with their existing Salesforce CRM, email infrastructure, and data enrichment tools.

Core capabilities deployed:

  • Lead Qualification Agent: Automatically scores, enriches, and routes inbound leads based on fit and intent signals
  • CRM Hygiene Agent: Continuously updates contact records, deduplicates entries, and flags incomplete data
  • Deal Progression Agent: Monitors pipeline, triggers follow-ups, and escalates stalled deals to sales leadership
  • Outreach Sequencing Agent: Personalizes email outreach based on prospect behavior and engagement patterns

The system was deployed in three phases over 90 days: pilot (30 days), expansion (30 days), and full production rollout (30 days). Integration was completed without disrupting existing sales workflows.
