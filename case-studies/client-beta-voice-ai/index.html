The Challenge

Call Volume Overwhelming Human Support Teams

Client Beta, a national telehealth provider serving 200,000+ patients, was drowning in support call volume. Their 85-person call center handled appointment scheduling, prescription refills, billing questions, and general support — but wait times averaged 18 minutes during peak hours.

The cost structure was unsustainable: $42 per call fully loaded, with 60% of calls handling routine, repeatable inquiries that required no clinical judgment. Patient satisfaction scores were declining, staff turnover exceeded 40% annually, and the company was facing a choice between hiring 50+ additional reps or turning away new patients.

Key pain points identified:

  • Average 18-minute wait time during business hours, leading to call abandonment
  • $42 fully-loaded cost per call with 60% handling routine inquiries
  • No after-hours support except for clinical emergencies
  • 40%+ annual turnover among call center staff due to repetitive work
  • Limited capacity to scale support without proportional cost increases
The Solution

Autonomous AI Voice Agents for Patient Support

Augentic AI designed and deployed a multi-agent voice AI system that automated routine patient support inquiries while seamlessly escalating complex issues to human representatives. The system integrated with Client Beta's EHR, scheduling platform, billing system, and telephony infrastructure.

Core capabilities deployed:

  • Appointment Scheduling Agent: Handles new appointments, rescheduling, cancellations, and sends confirmations via SMS
  • Prescription Refill Agent: Verifies patient identity, checks eligibility, and routes refill requests to pharmacy team
  • Billing Support Agent: Answers payment questions, explains charges, processes payments, and sets up payment plans
  • General Inquiry Agent: Provides information about services, hours, insurance, and routes clinical questions to nurses

The system was deployed in two phases over 60 days: pilot with 20% of inbound call volume (30 days), then full production rollout with human escalation protocols (30 days). Voice recognition accuracy exceeded 96% across diverse patient demographics and accents.

The Results

Transformational Cost Savings and Patient Experience Gains

Within 60 days of full deployment, Client Beta's AI voice system was handling 50,000+ calls per month with measurable improvements across cost, efficiency, and patient satisfaction metrics.
