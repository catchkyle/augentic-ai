## The Challenge

The fintech platform's sales team was closing $50M+ in ARR annually, but their revenue operations were stuck in spreadsheet hell. Every Monday morning, the VP of Sales spent hours manually pulling data from multiple systems to create a forecast for the board.

The manual process meant forecasts were often outdated by the time they were presented, leading to missed opportunities and resource allocation problems.

## Our Solution

We designed an autonomous AI agent that operates 24/7, continuously analyzing pipeline data and market signals. The system uses machine learning models trained on 3 years of historical deal data to predict close probability with unprecedented accuracy.

The agent doesn't just generate reports - it actively identifies patterns that humans miss, like subtle engagement drop-offs that signal deal risk.

## Impact & Results

The transformation was immediate. Within the first quarter, forecast accuracy jumped from 65% to 91%, giving the executive team unprecedented visibility into future revenue.

More importantly, the sales leadership team reclaimed 85 hours per month - time now spent coaching reps and closing strategic deals instead of wrangling spreadsheets.

The at-risk deal identification feature alone saved multiple six-figure deals that would have silently slipped away under the old manual system.
