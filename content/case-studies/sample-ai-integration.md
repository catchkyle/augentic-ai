A mid-market B2B SaaS company needed to scale their sales outreach without adding headcount. We built an autonomous AI system that qualifies inbound leads, enriches contact data, and initiates personalized outreach sequences.

The system integrates with HubSpot, Apollo, and their email infrastructure to operate continuously without manual intervention. Within 90 days of deployment, qualified lead volume increased by 3x while sales rep time spent on manual qualification dropped by 60%.
